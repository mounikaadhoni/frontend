About Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering

Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering is located in Vatluru, Eluru, Andhra Pradesh.

The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK).

Official Website: https://sircrrengg.ac.in

Available branches are:

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

Major recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro and Tech Mahindra.
